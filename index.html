How it works

Your browser. Your key. Your agent.

01

Drag nodes onto the canvas

Trigger, Claude LLM, HTTP request, Condition, Output. Connect the ports. That's the whole interface.

02

Pick your LLM

Run Ollama locally for free (Llama, Qwen, Phi) or paste your Anthropic API key for Claude. Stored in your browser's localStorage. Never touches our servers.

03

Run, export, share

Test the agent live. Export to JSON. Share it, sell it on the marketplace, or fork it on GitHub.
